Research Activities


CURRENT SPONSORED PROJECTS

  1. Project Title-Design of novel bifunctional gold-Ti- and Fe-modified zeolite functional materials for the catalytic oxidation of hydrocarbons.

    2. India-Netherland bi-lateral project

    Principal Investigator: Dr.Biswajit Chowdhury

    Sponsoring Agency:DST, Govt. of India


  2. Project Title-Mesoporous Mixed Oxide Supported Gold Nanoparticle for Oxidant free Dehydrogenation Reaction.

    3. Principal Investigator: Dr.Biswajit Chowdhury

    Sponsoring Agency: DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  3. Project Title-Development of Organic-Inorganic hybrid nanocomposites for Vapour phase Beckmann Rearrangement Reaction

    4. Principal Investigator: Dr.Biswajit Chowdhury

    Sponsoring Agency:UGC, New Delhi.


  4. Project Title-Synthesis, characterization of discrete metal organic assemblies of dipodal and tripodal ligands and their potential application in selective molecular recognition and catalysis.

    5. Principal Investigator: Dr. Rohit P. John

    Sponsoring Agency:DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  5. Project Title-Polyacrylamide grafted hydroxypropyl methyl cellulose (HPMC-g PAM): Development and application as an efficient polymeric flocculant and viscosifier.

    6. Principal Investigator: Dr. Sagar Pal

    Sponsoring Agency:DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  6. Project Title-Amphoteric xanthan gum (AXG): Synthesis, characterization and application as a novel polymeric flocculant for the treatment of industrial wastewater.

    7. Principal Investigator: Dr. Sagar Pal

    Sponsoring Agency: DST (TSG), Govt. of India.


  7. Project Title-High performance multifunctional guar gum for wastewater and Industrial effluent treatment.

    8. Principal Investigator:Dr. Sagar Pal

    Sponsoring Agency:DST (WTI), Govt. of India.


  8. Project Title-Molecular Recognition and Applications of Fluorescence Sensors in Host Guest Chemistry

    9. Principal Investigator: Dr. Swapan Dey

    Sponsoring Agency: DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  9. Project Title-A photochemical approach to surfactant free metal nano particles & nanoclusters and their catalytic applications in organic synthesis

    10. Principal Investigator: Dr. Somnath Yadav

    Sponsoring Agency:DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  10. Project Title-Synthesis of Triplesalen-based metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) and investigation on their functional applicability.

    11. Principal Investigator: Dr. Hari Pada Nayek

    Sponsoring Agency: DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  11. Project Title- A study of polymeric layer on efficiency enhancement of Upconversion Nanoparticles for Biomedical applications (Recommended for funding)

    12. Principal Investigator:Dr. S. K. Sahu

    Sponsoring Agency:DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  12. Project Title-Synthesis and Functionalization of Graphene for controlled dispersion in polymer blends. (Recommended for funding)

    Sponsoring Agency:DST (SERB), Govt. of India.


  13. Project Title-Biodegradable imprinted nanomaterials: A unique combination of selectivity, sensitivity and biocompatibility (Recommended for funding)

    14. Principal Investigator: Dr. Rashmi Madhuri

    Sponsoring Agency: DST (SERB), Govt. of India



The department has also undertaken research projects in various areas like :

  • Characterization and utilization of fly ash
  • Development of corrosion inhibitors for casing steel used in oil wells during acidization
  • Prevention of scale formation in oil wells
  • Beneficiation of china clay
  • Thermal analysis
  • Organometallic compounds as chiral catalysts
  • Synthesis of frother reagents
  • Porous and nanomaterials for using as heterogeneous catalysts for different reactions Presently there are seven full time Ph.D students and a number of part time scholars working in the department.
