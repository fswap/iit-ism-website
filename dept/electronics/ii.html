Industrial Interaction

As the E & I department is relatively new as a full fledged department, the present emphasis is on building the R & D infrastructure through R & D projects. The R & D projects are on the state of art technology and of great industrial applications. In immediate future, the consultancy services offered by the department will be in the following areas:

Microelectronics

Development of various types of current mode filters, oscillators and other circuits for communication, VLSI etc.

Optical Communication

Fabrication of EDF Tunable Broadband Source
EDF Based Optical Amplifiers for C – & L – Band
Optical Modulators and Isolators.

Microwave, Antenna & RF circuits

Phased array antenna,
Ground penetrating Radar for water pocket detection in coal mines
Mine detection in battlefields,
RF measurements for mobile communication.
EMI/EMC measurement in network analyser,
Power line communication for internet access
Electromagnetic pulse sensor design
